Chemical peels


Chemical peels sound quite daunting but really are a very safe an effective way of exfoliating the skin using "chemical" ingredients that are usually derrived and sourced naturally such as lactic acid from milk. Some common ingredients used in chemical peels include lactic acid, glycolic acid, salicylic acid, phytic acid and many more. All of these ingredients a=have different efects on the skin so depending on what your concerns are, your practitioner will choose the best suited peel for you. Chemical peels vary in strength, but regular medium strength chemical peels, can be done every 4-6 weeks and will fantastic results when done regularly.


What to expect


Chemical peels are relatively comfortable treatments to have. You will feel a warm to a strong tingling sensation that will last for about 3-5 minutes per peel application. The skin may be a little warm to touch for about an hour after your treatment. You may have some slight pinkness after your peel, which will last for about an hour. Peeling of the skin does not occur until about 3 days post peel. This can be anything from a general feeling of dryness on the skin to visible peeling and will last for around 3-5 days. Its not something that other people will notice and you can go about you days as normal. Once the peeling has stopped, your skin will feel and look fresher, brighter, more hydrated. It usually takes a week to see final results from your peel treatment.


Considerations


  1. Avoid exfoliating indredients and retinol for 5 days prior to treatment
  2. SPF is essential after treatment as the skin is peeling
  3. no waxing the are 5 days prior or post treatment
  4. No heat treatments or UV exposure for 5 days prior or post treatment
  5. No exercise for 24 hours post treatment
  6. No picking or forcefully peeling the skin
