About River Medical


With 15 years of knoweldge and experience, River Medical is Ireland's leading, medically led aesthetics clinic. We are expert and trusted providers of non surgical cosmetic procedures.
Our expert team of medically led skin specialists welcome you for a consultation to address your skin concerns. Our General Registered Nurses and Advances Skin Specialists have over 50 years experience combined and a wealth of knowledge to help you achieve the results you are looking for. They enjoy attending regular industry training to apply the best advancements in technology and products to their patients and love nothing more than seeing happy patients back for reassessments.
Our patient liaison officers are on hand to book you in today for your free consultation or answer any questions you may have. We have a variety of treatments to help make you feel and look your very best, no matter what your concern is. Our medically led skin specialists are on hand to treat everything from acne and pigmentation to roecea and ageing. Even if you just want to feel fresh and glowy, we have a range of treatments to choose from. Call or email now to book your one to one tailored consulattion with one of our skin professionals today.
