Secret RF


Secret RF is a form of microneedling with radio frequncy.The microneedles can be adjusted to correct dept of thr area we are working on and are used as a mode of trasport for the radio frequency.Radio frquency will emmit heat to the skin, in turn, tightening the tissues.


What to expect


The treatment takes about 45 minutes. We apply numbing cream to the skin for extra comfort. After the tretment your skin will feel hot and look red for the rest of the day. You will leave with a post treatment kit which is reccomended to be used for 5-7 days post treatment. It is recommended to have a course of 3-4 treatments to improve skin tightness, however you will see a small result after one treatment.


Considerations


  1. Avoid exfoliating indredients and retinol for 5 days prior to treatment.
  2. SPF is essential after treatment
  3. no waxing the are 5 days prior or post treatment
  4. No exercise for 24 hours post treatment
  5. Only apply products that have been recommended to you by your practitioner.
  6. No heat treatments or UV exposure for 5 days prior or post treatment
